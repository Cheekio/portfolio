Thanks for your interest in gleaning!

Before you head out to farms, we’d like to know how best to contact you, where you’re interested in gleaning, and your emergency contact.

Enter a username for logging in and posting comments to the rideshare board –choose something you’re comfortable with other registered volunteers viewing.

How and when will I be contacted?

After filling out this form you’ll receive a confirmation email from the Interface – but gleaning organizations cannot email you until after you RSVP for an event they’re hosting…and they cannot view any of your contact information until after you actually attend a glean. Only once you’ve put in hours with an organization may they send additional correspondence.

We also ask that every volunteer sign a waiver of liability and volunteer agreement before entering any farm fields – please read the text below and consider whether you can commit to these agreements.
